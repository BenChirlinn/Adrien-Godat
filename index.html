Adrien Godat is a producer, musical director, and session/touring guitarist currently based in New York City.

A recent graduate of the Berklee College of Music, Adrien is the musical director and lead guitarist of the 1x5 Showcase at Rockwood Music Hall in NYC – a regular live music event he created and continues to book and organize himself. 1x5 has featured some of New York's most talented musicians and songwriters, including several top ten finalists of NBC's "The Voice."

As a guitarist, Adrien has performed live with over 30 artists; including world music star Rokia Traore. He regularly performs with Anthony Hall, for whom he is the musical director.

As a producer, Adrien's work can be heard on the debut albums of Phillip Noss ("Lover on the Run"), Owen Danoff ("Twelve Stories"), and Mike Squillante.

Adrien created and manages Agora Sounds LLC, which operates as both a record label and a multimedia production company. Check us out here: www.agorasounds.com
